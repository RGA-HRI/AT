Doctoral program in Mathematics at HRI :

    The program begins with compulsory courseworks and project, followed by research work leading to a Ph.D. degree. The degree is granted by the Homi Bhabha National Institute (HBNI).
    The page is primarily for the Graduate Courseworks in Analysis at HRI.


Course Curriculums:

  • Semester I
      Measure Theory, Fourier Analysis, Calculus on normed linear spaces, Introduction to Calculus of variation.
  • Semester II
      Functional Analysis, Topological Vector Spaces, Spectral Theory, Distribution Theory.
  • Semester III (Optional)
      Sobolev Spaces, L^2 theory of linear elliptic equations, regularity results.
      Linear evolution equations, Galerkin's method, Semigroup theory.
