Doctoral program in Mathematics at HRI :

    The program begins with compulsory courseworks and project, followed by research work leading to a Ph.D. degree. The degree is granted by the Homi Bhabha National Institute (HBNI).
    The page is primarily for the Graduate Courseworks in Analysis at HRI.


Course Curriculums:

    Measure Theory
    Abstract measure theory: \sigma-algebra, definition and examples of measure spaces, Borel sets, Caratheodory extension, completion of measure spaces. Construction of Lebesgue and Lebesgue-Stieltjes measures on R. Measurable functions, Lebesgue integration, convergence theorems: Fatou's lemma, Monotone and Dominated Convergence Theorem. Product measure, Fubini's Theorem. Lebesgue decomposition, Radon-Nikodym Theorem. Absolutely continuous functions, fundamental theorem of calculus.
    L^p spaces, duality, Lebesgue differentiation Theorem, convolution, Young's inequality.
    Fourier transform
    Fourier transform in L^1. Schwartz space, convolution and product formulas, differentiation and polynomial multiplication formulas, Fourier inversion, Plancherel Theorem, Paley-Wiener Theorem. Fourier transform in L^p spaces 1 < p <= 2.
\textbf{Calculus on normed linear spaces}{[}8 Lectures{]} Normed vector spaces and Banach spaces. Maps between Banach spaces, its continuity and differentiability. The Fr\'{e}chet differential, properties of the Fr\'{e}chet derivative. Partial differentiation in a finite dimensional real vector space. Mean value theorem and applications. Inverse function theorem, Implicit function theorem. Method of Lagrange mulitpliers. Critical point analysis for several variables: Taylor’s multinomial for many-variables, second derivative test, quadratic forms.   • Lecture Series by Prof. S. Thangavelu: "Bargmann and Fock meet Hardy: An uncertainty principle for operators"
      • In these lectures, we discuss certain bounded linear operators on Fock/twisted Fock spaces leading to the definition of twisted Heisenberg groups $ H^n_\lambda(\C)$. We construct a certain unitary representation of this group and use an operator analogue of Hardy's theorem proves that this representation is irreducible. --:References:--

      Valentine "Valya" Bargmann (April 6, 1908 – July 20, 1989) was a German-American mathematician and theoretical physicist.
      Vladimir Aleksandrovich Fock (December 22, 1898 – December 27, 1974) was a Soviet physicist, who did foundational work on quantum mechanics and quantum electrodynamics.
      Godfrey Harold Hardy (7 February 1877 – 1 December 1947) was an English mathematician, known for his achievements in number theory and mathematical analysis.
